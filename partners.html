Methodology

Savimbo & Cercarbono

Our work is grounded in the Savimbo Indicator Species Biodiversity Methodology (ISBM), co-developed by Indigenous stewards in the Colombian Amazon. InSight Biodiversity is not affiliated with Savimbo financially — we are independent practitioners who believe in the methodology's potential for BC and work to adapt it under Indigenous governance in this region.

Biodiversity credits generated through ISBM-based pilots are certified through the Cercarbono Biodiversity Certification Programme (CBCP) — the world's first certified biodiversity credit standard. Savimbo ISBM-based projects have generated the first CBCP-certified biodiversity credits available for international trading.

Savimbo

Indigenous-designed ISBM methodology. Co-developed by the Kamentša, Cofan, Pasto, Emberá Chami, and Inga peoples of the Colombian Amazon.

isbm.savimbo.com →

Cercarbono

Certification body for the world's first biodiversity credit standard (CBCP). Verifies and certifies ISBM-based biodiversity credits for international trading.

cercarbono.com →
