Methodology

Savimbo & Cercarbono

Our work is grounded in the Savimbo Indicator Species Biodiversity Methodology (ISBM), co-developed by Indigenous stewards in the Colombian Amazon. InSight Biodiversity is not affiliated with Savimbo financially — we are independent practitioners adapting it under Indigenous governance in BC.

Savimbo

Indigenous-designed ISBM methodology. Co-developed by the Kamentša, Cofan, Pasto, Emberá Chami, and Inga peoples of the Colombian Amazon.

isbm.savimbo.com →

Cercarbono

Certification body for the world's first biodiversity credit standard (CBCP). Verifies and certifies ISBM-based biodiversity credits for international trading.

cercarbono.com →
